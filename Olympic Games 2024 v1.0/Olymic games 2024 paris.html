2024 Olympic Games in Paris

About the Event

The 2024 Olympic Games will be held in Paris. This will be the third time Paris hosts the Olympics, having previously done so in 1900 and 1924. The games are expected to be a grand event with athletes from around the world participating.

The schedule for events and activities will be published closer to the date of the games. Stay tuned for updates on our website!
